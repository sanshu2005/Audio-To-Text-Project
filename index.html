From microphone signal to useful action

The converter is designed as a practical assistant pipeline: each layer improves the signal, narrows uncertainty, and produces structured output that software can trust.

1

Audio Capture

Record microphone input, stream audio chunks, and detect when a person is speaking.

2

Signal Cleanup

Reduce noise, normalize volume, trim silence, and prepare a stable signal for recognition.

3

Speech-to-Text

Use an ASR model to produce transcripts with punctuation, timing, and confidence scores.

4

NLP Understanding

Classify intent, extract entities, resolve context, and handle ambiguous requests.

5

Assistant Action

Trigger APIs, smart devices, reminders, searches, or a spoken confirmation response.
